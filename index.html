Vendor Ecosystem Optimization

The core strength of this strategy is the deep integration of our expanded line card (including XDR and GRC leaders) across all five revenue bundles.

  • Microsoft/Cloud (M365, Azure, AWS): Anchors five out of five bundles.
  • Backup & DR (Veeam/Commvault/VMware): Core to resilience and infrastructure bundles (B1, B2).
  • AI Security & XDR (CP, Sophos, CS, S1, B-def): Provides the advanced security layer across all bundles (B1, B2, B4, B5).
  • GRC & Compliance (Scorecard, Apptega): Drives high-margin consulting revenue in B3 and B5.
  • Ops & Enablers (QuickBooks, BitTitan, Nord): Critical for migration, finance, and secure access across the portfolio.
